  4. Restart your Minecraft server.

HealLC is a Minecraft 1.20 Spigot plugin made in Java. It is a heal plugin designed to regenerate players' health to 20.

Permissions

  • heal.use: Heal yourself.
  • healall.use: Heal every online player.
  • healother.use: Heal other players.

Commands

  • /heal: Heal yourself.
  • /healall: Heal every online player.
  • /heal <PLAYER>: Heal other players.
